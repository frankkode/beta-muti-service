We are known for reliable delivery services, moving in and out, Environmental services and wellbeing consultations all over the world. Our Mission

OUR objective is to become an extension of our clients' business by taking care of all their courier needs by providing them with unparallel transportation management solutions acceding to our core values of reliability, accuracy, integrity, and service. We will never fail in exceeding our clients’ expectations. We are specialized in moving in & moving out services, scheduled deliveries, and environmental solutions:

  • We guarantee the integrity of your supply chain.
  • We concentrate on less than 24-hour deliveries.
  • We deliver same-day – even within an hour for rush critical deliveries.
  • We are a highly-specialized, customer-driven business, focused on moving in and moving out services,moving for customers on a “just-in-time” basis.

Our Vision

WE want to become the premiere Same-day-delivery company serving the courier needs of businesses and individuals in Finland . Further, in a couple of years we'll extend our reach to the whole nation and beyond. We want to realize our objectives by providing our clients with consistent, dependable and accurate service at a cost effective rate.
